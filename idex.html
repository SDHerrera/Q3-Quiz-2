Metro Manila Film Festival Review 2023



Movie 1: GomBurZa

Synopsis

GomBurZa is a historical film that will move and inspire today's Filipinos. It tells the story of three martyr priests - Mariano Gomez (Dante Rivero), Jose Burgos (Cedrick Juan), and Jacinto Zamora (Enchong Dee) - who were executed by Spain in 1872 falsely accused of sedition and treason.

Cast & Directors:
Mariano Gomez (Dante Rivero)
Jose Burgos (Cedrick Juan)
Jacinto Zamora (Enchong Dee)

Movie Reviews

⭐⭐⭐⭐

- 'Marred by its winding structure, Gomburza, in this retelling, feels just as elusive as this thing we loosely refer to as national identity'

Movie 2: Rewind

Synopsis

John (Dingdong Dantes) is a businessman who is always on the go, to the detriment of his relationships with his loving wife Mary (Marian Rivera) and his son Austin (Jordan Lim.) It all comes to a head when several unfortunate events befall John, leading to a car accident that takes the life of his wife. It is there where he meets a certain quirky carpenter with time travel powers (Pepe Herrera) who allows him to live the day before the accident – but at a substantial personal cost…

Cast & Directors:
Dingdong Dantes (John Nuñez)
Marian Rivera (Mary Nuñez)
Pepe Herrera (Jess)

Movie Reviews

⭐⭐⭐

- 'A thrilling and emotional rollercoaster that will keep you at the edge of your seat until the very end.'

Movie 3: Firefly

Synopsis

Set in 1999, Firefly is told from the perspective of an adult Tonton (Dingdong Dantes) who reminisces about his childhood. A ten-year old Tonton (Euwenn Mikaell) goes on a search for the "Island of Fireflies", a mystical island he learned from Elay, his mother (Alessandra De Rossi) during bedtime stories.

Cast & Directors:
Adult Tonton (Dingdong Dantes)
Carmen Alcantara (Yayo Aguila)
Mariela "Elay" Alvaro (Alessandra De Rossi)

Movie Reviews

⭐⭐⭐⭐

- 'TFirefly is a film that celebrates the storytellers and the listeners, the dreamers and the doers, the lovers and the loved ones."'
